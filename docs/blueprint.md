# **App Name**: NIT Agartala CEWN

## Core Features:

- Emergency SOS System: One-tap emergency alerts with location and student details shared with campus security, wardens, and the campus hospital.
- Campus Hospital Transparency System: Tracks waiting times, doctor availability, and collects post-visit feedback to improve hospital performance.
- Mess Food Safety & Health Monitor: Allows students to rate daily food quality, report feeling sick after meals, and alerts relevant authorities to potential food poisoning clusters.
- Admin Dashboard: Provides live emergency alerts, hospital response time graphs, mess hygiene heatmaps, and student health trend analysis for administrators.
- Priority Escalation System: Automatically escalates emergency alerts based on predefined rules and response times, ensuring timely intervention.
- Predictive Health Insights: Uses generative AI to analyze student health data and identify potential health risks. LLM will use data collected from emergency reports, hospital feedback, and mess food ratings as tools to find risks.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#293B5F) to convey trust and reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#E7E8EA) to provide a clean and accessible interface.
- Accent color: Vivid orange (#D36B00) for emergency alerts and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable user interface.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons for each module and feature to enhance usability.
- Prioritize key information and emergency functions with a simple and intuitive layout.
- Use subtle animations and transitions to provide feedback and guide users through the application.
